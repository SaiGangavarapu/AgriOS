# What Changed — Compliance, Certification, Inspection, Scheme Eligibility, and Regulatory Reporting Core v1.0

Added the Compliance bounded context.

The implementation models standards, requirements, obligations, certification
applications, inspections, findings, and farmer scheme eligibility.

Scheme eligibility is deterministic and explainable. Assessments store the
rule version, reason codes, missing information, and evidence snapshot instead
of presenting eligibility as a guaranteed government decision.

Regulatory report and audit schemas are provider-neutral foundations for future
authority-specific adapters.
