# Patch 004 — Agriculture Platform BRD Part 2

## Purpose

Patch 004 expands the AgriOS Business Requirements Document with farmer onboarding, household and organization relationships, farm and field registration, land tenure, water sources, roles, consent, assisted onboarding, field-officer workflows, and related business rules.

## Included documents

- Farmer onboarding
- Farmer household and organization relationships
- Farm registration
- Field registration and boundary capture
- Land tenure and occupancy
- Water-source management
- Role and access requirements
- Consent and data-sharing requirements
- Field-officer and assisted workflows
- Validation and exception rules
- Reporting and audit requirements
- Traceability register expansion

## Apply

Extract this ZIP into the AgriOS repository root. All paths are repository-relative and there is no wrapper directory.

`Add AgriOS BRD part 2`
