# AgriOS Farmer Finance, Cost Accounting, Profitability, and Income Aggregation Core v1.0

Implemented:

- immutable financial-event ingestion with idempotency
- farm-income and farm-expense events from internal and external modules
- chart-of-accounts and journal schema foundation
- double-entry journal-entry and journal-line schema
- cost centers and cost allocation
- crop-cycle, field, and farmer financial dimensions
- seasonal budgets and budget lines
- budget approval
- farmer profitability calculation
- revenue, operating cost, overhead, depreciation, finance cost, net profit, ROI, and cost per unit
- income aggregation across crop sales, livestock, dairy, marketplace, seller ERP, subsidy, insurance, and other income
- farm asset, loan, subsidy, and insurance database foundations
- Flyway migrations V031 through V033
