# Patch 006 — Agriculture Platform BRD Part 4

## Purpose

Patch 006 expands the AgriOS Business Requirements Document with nutrient management, irrigation scheduling, weather and monsoon intelligence, IoT device management, telemetry, alerts, automation safeguards, expert advisory workflows, and related governance.

## Included documents

- Nutrient-management planning
- Fertilizer and organic-input application controls
- Irrigation planning and scheduling
- Weather-data and forecast management
- Monsoon and climate-risk advisories
- IoT device registry and installation
- Telemetry ingestion and quality
- Device alerts and maintenance
- Automation and actuator safeguards
- Advisory generation and explanation
- Expert review and consultation
- Validation, reporting, and audit requirements
- Traceability register expansion

## Apply

Extract this ZIP into the AgriOS repository root. All archive paths are repository-relative and there is no wrapper directory.
