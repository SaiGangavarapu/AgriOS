# What Changed — Pest, Disease, Crop Health, and IPM Core v1.0

Added the Crop Health bounded context.

The design follows integrated pest management principles:

1. identify and monitor
2. compare observations against thresholds
3. prefer prevention and non-chemical controls
4. use chemical controls only as a governed last resort
5. verify safety conditions before execution
6. review effectiveness after intervention

The crop-health score is deterministic and evidence-backed.
