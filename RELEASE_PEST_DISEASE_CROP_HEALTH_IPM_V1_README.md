# AgriOS Pest, Disease, Crop Health, and Integrated Pest Management Core v1.0

Implemented:

- pest catalog
- disease catalog
- crop-specific symptoms and favorable conditions
- scouting visits
- pest, disease, deficiency, weed, abiotic-stress, and beneficial-insect observations
- incidence, severity, population, confidence, and evidence capture
- crop-health assessments
- pest-pressure and disease-pressure scoring
- outbreak records
- IPM plans
- prevention, monitoring, cultural, mechanical, physical, biological, botanical,
  behavioral, sanitation, and chemical-last-resort actions
- pre-harvest interval, re-entry interval, pollinator warning, and water-body buffer fields
- IPM execution with weather and safety validation
- Flyway migrations V025 through V027
