# Patch 005 — Agriculture Platform BRD Part 3

## Purpose

Patch 005 expands the AgriOS Business Requirements Document with soil and water testing, crop and variety knowledge, crop suitability, crop planning, crop-cycle management, growth stages, seed management, farm operations, crop calendars, and season-closure requirements.

## Included documents

- Soil-sampling and laboratory workflows
- Water-testing and quality workflows
- Crop and variety knowledge management
- Crop suitability and ranking
- Crop-plan creation and approval
- Crop-cycle lifecycle
- Growth-stage management
- Seed and planting-material management
- Farm-operation planning and recording
- Crop-calendar and task workflows
- Season closure and learning
- Validation, reporting, and audit requirements
- Traceability register expansion

## Apply

Extract this ZIP into the AgriOS repository root. All archive paths are repository-relative and there is no wrapper directory.
