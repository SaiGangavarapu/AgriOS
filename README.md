# AgriOS

AgriOS is an India-first, extensible agriculture operating platform intended to support farmers, agronomists, field officers, laboratories, input providers, buyers, insurers, financial institutions, researchers, and government partners.

The platform will combine:

- Farm and field registration
- Soil and water intelligence
- Crop and seed suitability
- Natural, organic, integrated, and conventional farming plans
- Weather and monsoon intelligence
- IoT-based field monitoring
- Irrigation and nutrient decision support
- Pest and disease surveillance
- Market intelligence
- Farmer task calendars
- Expert review workflows
- AI-assisted farmer interaction
- Offline-first mobile access
- Consent-based data sharing
