# Patch 008 — Agriculture Platform BRD Part 6

## Purpose

Patch 008 completes the cross-cutting Business Requirements Document foundation for AgriOS. It covers multilingual user experience, voice interaction, offline operation, notifications, security, privacy, compliance, administration, analytics, support, platform operations, and non-functional business expectations.

## Included documents

- Multilingual and accessibility requirements
- Voice and conversational interaction
- Offline-first operation and synchronization
- Notification and communication channels
- Identity, authentication, and session requirements
- Authorization and segregation of duties
- Privacy, consent, retention, and data-subject rights
- Security monitoring and incident response
- Compliance and policy administration
- Platform configuration and master-data administration
- Reporting, analytics, and research governance
- Support, service management, and field operations
- Availability, continuity, backup, and disaster recovery
- Performance, scalability, and interoperability
- BRD completion review and open decisions
- Traceability register expansion

## Apply

Extract this ZIP into the AgriOS repository root. All archive paths are repository-relative and there is no wrapper directory.
