# Patch 007 — Agriculture Platform BRD Part 5

## Purpose

Patch 007 expands the AgriOS Business Requirements Document with pest and disease management, scouting, image-based observations, treatment governance, harvest, post-harvest, storage, market intelligence, buyer workflows, farm economics, insurance, lending, and produce traceability.

## Included documents

- Pest and disease knowledge governance
- Field scouting and symptom observations
- Image-based crop-health observations
- Diagnosis confidence and expert escalation
- Treatment planning and safety governance
- Harvest planning and execution
- Post-harvest, grading, and storage
- Market intelligence and buyer workflows
- Farm economics and profitability
- Insurance and claim-support workflows
- Lending and consented financial evidence
- Produce lot traceability
- Validation, reporting, and audit requirements
- Traceability register expansion

## Apply

Extract this ZIP into the AgriOS repository root. All archive paths are repository-relative and there is no wrapper directory.
