# What Changed — Soil, Water, Crop Knowledge, and Crop Planning Core v1.0

Added executable backend capabilities and physical schemas for:

1. Soil and water laboratory workflows
2. Current field and water-source analytical profiles
3. Governed crop and variety knowledge
4. Crop requirement constraints
5. Crop suitability scoring
6. Crop plan creation and approval

The crop suitability algorithm is deliberately deterministic and transparent.
It does not use an LLM to generate agronomic scores.
