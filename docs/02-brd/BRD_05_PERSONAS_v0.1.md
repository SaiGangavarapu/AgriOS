# BRD 05 — Personas

## P-01: Smallholder farmer

Characteristics:

- limited land
- limited working capital
- family labour
- Android phone
- variable connectivity
- preference for local language and voice

Needs:

- simple daily tasks
- weather warnings
- affordable crop plans
- low-risk recommendations
- offline access
- human help when uncertain

## P-02: Progressive farmer

Characteristics:

- multiple fields
- greater willingness to adopt technology
- may use drip irrigation, machinery, or sensors

Needs:

- field comparison
- profitability analysis
- sensor integration
- crop planning
- advanced alerts
- exportable reports

## P-03: Tenant farmer

Characteristics:

- may lack formal land records
- shorter planning horizon
- limited control over long-term soil investment

Needs:

- lease-aware farm records
- privacy
- seasonal planning
- low-capital options
- proof of operations and production

## P-04: Woman farmer or household operator

Characteristics:

- may perform major farming work while formal ownership is recorded elsewhere
- may share device access

Needs:

- inclusive registration
- delegated access
- simple task flows
- voice and local language
- recognition of operational role

## P-05: Field officer

Needs:

- assigned farmer list
- route planning
- offline forms
- visit records
- issue escalation
- photo and geo evidence
- pending follow-up dashboard

## P-06: Agronomist

Needs:

- crop and field context
- soil and weather data
- observation history
- recommendation draft
- evidence references
- approval and revision controls

## P-07: Laboratory operator

Needs:

- sample receipt
- chain of custody
- test method
- result entry or import
- correction workflow
- report generation

## P-08: FPO manager

Needs:

- member onboarding
- aggregate crop plans
- input demand
- harvest forecast
- advisory reach
- buyer coordination
- consent-aware reporting

## P-09: Input seller

Needs:

- inventory and product information
- farmer order support
- clear separation from independent recommendations

## P-10: Buyer or aggregator

Needs:

- expected harvest
- quality and grade
- lot traceability
- logistics coordination
- consented farmer information

## P-11: Insurer or lender

Needs:

- verified source metadata
- consent
- field and crop records
- risk indicators
- clear distinction between observation and self-declaration
