# BRD 17 — Roles and Delegated Access

## 1. Objective

Provide least-privilege access while supporting real-world shared farming operations.

## 2. Primary roles

- farmer
- household operator
- farm worker
- field officer
- agronomist
- laboratory operator
- FPO manager
- buyer
- insurer
- lender
- administrator
- support operator

## 3. Access dimensions

Access should be constrained by:

- role
- organization
- farm
- field
- service
- action
- time period
- consent
- verification level

## 4. Delegation

A farmer may delegate:

- task completion
- operation recording
- photo upload
- field visit support
- cost entry
- advisory acknowledgement
- report viewing

Sensitive actions require stronger authority:

- consent grant or revocation
- sharing with financial institution
- tenure evidence access
- profile merge
- account suspension

## 5. Time-bound access

Temporary access should support:

- field visit
- consultation
- seasonal worker
- audit
- claim inspection

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-051: The platform shall enforce least-privilege access.
- BR-052: Access shall be scoped to farms, fields, services, and time where applicable.
- BR-053: Delegated access shall be revocable and auditable.
- BR-054: Sensitive actions shall require elevated authority.
- BR-055: Temporary access shall expire automatically.
