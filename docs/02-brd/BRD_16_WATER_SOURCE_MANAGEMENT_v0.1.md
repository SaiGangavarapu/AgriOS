# BRD 16 — Water-Source Management

## 1. Objective

Register and monitor water availability, reliability, quality, and access rights for crop planning and irrigation decisions.

## 2. Water-source types

- borewell
- open well
- canal
- tank
- pond
- reservoir
- harvested rainwater
- river or stream
- municipal or treated source where permitted
- shared irrigation source

## 3. Water-source profile

- source type
- location
- linked farm or fields
- ownership
- access arrangement
- seasonal availability
- estimated capacity or flow
- pumping system
- energy source
- storage
- irrigation method
- water-quality status
- restrictions
- last inspection

## 4. Reliability classification

- reliable
- seasonal
- intermittent
- emergency-only
- unknown

## 5. Shared sources

The platform may record:

- participating farmers
- allocation
- schedule
- dispute status
- operator
- maintenance responsibility

## 6. Water-quality evidence

- sample
- laboratory
- test date
- method
- salinity indicators
- sodicity indicators
- contamination warnings
- validity period

## 7. Business requirements

- BR-046: The platform shall support multiple water sources per farm.
- BR-047: Water-source reliability shall influence crop planning.
- BR-048: Water quality shall be linked to relevant fields and crops.
- BR-049: Shared water sources shall support allocation and access records.
- BR-050: Expired or missing water-quality data shall reduce recommendation confidence.
