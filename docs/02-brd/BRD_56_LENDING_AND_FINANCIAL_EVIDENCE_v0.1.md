# BRD 56 — Lending and Financial Evidence

## 1. Objective

Allow farmers to share consented farm-performance and planning evidence with lenders while preventing misleading or excessive disclosure.

## 2. Potential evidence

- verified farmer profile
- farm and field records
- crop plan
- expected input budget
- historical crop cycles
- yield records
- sales records
- insurance status
- organization membership
- expert-reviewed advisories
- consented transaction history

## 3. Evidence quality

Each item shall indicate:

- source
- date
- verification level
- confidence
- completeness
- correction history
- self-declared status

## 4. Sharing workflow

`Farmer Selects Purpose → Reviews Data Scope → Grants Consent → Evidence Package Generated → Shared → Access Expires/Revoked`

## 5. Restrictions

- no hidden scoring
- no automatic loan rejection by AgriOS
- no unrelated data sharing
- no inference of legal ownership from cultivation records
- no permanent lender access without valid consent

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-248: The platform shall generate consented financial-evidence packages.
- BR-249: Evidence shall expose source, verification, confidence, and date.
- BR-250: Farmers shall review the data scope before sharing.
- BR-251: Shared access shall expire or be revocable.
- BR-252: AgriOS shall not make autonomous credit decisions.
