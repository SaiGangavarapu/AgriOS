# BRD 18 — Consent and Data Sharing

## 1. Objective

Ensure that farmers understand and control how their data is collected, used, and shared.

## 2. Consent purposes

Separate consent should be available for:

- agronomy advisory
- weather alerts
- IoT telemetry
- research
- insurance
- lending
- buyer traceability
- government programme
- marketing
- anonymized analytics

## 3. Consent properties

- purpose
- data categories
- recipient
- geographic or field scope
- valid-from
- expiry
- revocation
- channel
- language
- evidence of acceptance
- policy version

## 4. Consent principles

- specific
- informed
- understandable
- revocable
- time-bound where appropriate
- minimal
- auditable

## 5. Data categories

- identity
- contact
- farm and field
- tenure
- soil and water
- crop and operation
- financial
- device telemetry
- location
- images
- expert consultation
- market transaction

## 6. Revocation

Revocation must:

- stop future sharing
- preserve legally required audit evidence
- not silently delete historical transactions
- notify dependent services where needed

## 7. Business requirements

- BR-056: The platform shall maintain purpose-specific consent.
- BR-057: Consent shall reference data categories and recipients.
- BR-058: The platform shall support revocation and expiry.
- BR-059: Data sharing shall be blocked when required consent is absent.
- BR-060: Consent text shall be available in the farmer's selected language.
