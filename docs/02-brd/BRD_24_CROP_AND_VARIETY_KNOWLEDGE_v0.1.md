# BRD 24 — Crop and Variety Knowledge

## 1. Objective

Maintain a governed, versioned, and geographically scoped catalogue of crops and varieties.

## 2. Crop knowledge

- crop name
- scientific name
- crop group
- season
- duration
- growth stages
- temperature range
- rainfall requirement
- soil suitability
- pH tolerance
- salinity tolerance
- drainage requirement
- root depth
- nutrient demand
- water demand
- major pests
- major diseases
- harvest indicators
- post-harvest requirements

## 3. Variety knowledge

- variety name
- crop
- breeder or institution
- release or notification status
- recommended geography
- maturity
- sowing window
- irrigation suitability
- quality traits
- resistance and tolerance traits
- yield evidence
- market preference
- seed availability status

## 4. Governance

Knowledge status:

`Draft → Expert Reviewed → Approved → Published → Superseded`

## 5. Applicability

Every knowledge item shall indicate:

- geography
- soil
- season
- irrigation condition
- farming system
- source
- evidence grade
- valid-from
- review date

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-087: The platform shall maintain crop and variety catalogues.
- BR-088: Knowledge shall be versioned and evidence-linked.
- BR-089: Published knowledge shall be geographically scoped.
- BR-090: Superseded knowledge shall remain auditable.
- BR-091: Commercial claims shall be distinguishable from independent evidence.
