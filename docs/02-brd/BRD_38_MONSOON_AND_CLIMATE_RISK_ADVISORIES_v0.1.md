# BRD 38 — Monsoon and Climate-Risk Advisories

## 1. Objective

Convert monsoon progression, seasonal outlooks, current observations, and weather risks into practical agricultural guidance.

## 2. Monsoon information

- climatological onset range
- official seasonal outlook
- current progression
- local rainfall accumulation
- effective rainfall
- soil-moisture readiness
- sowing-window confidence
- withdrawal indicators
- delayed-onset contingency

## 3. Supported risks

- delayed onset
- false onset
- early dry spell
- prolonged dry spell
- heavy rain
- waterlogging
- flood
- heat stress
- cold or frost
- high wind
- hail
- harvest rain
- high humidity and drying risk

## 4. Advisory examples

- delay sowing
- switch to shorter-duration crop or variety
- preserve seed for re-sowing
- open drainage channels
- postpone fertilizer application
- skip irrigation
- protect nursery
- advance or delay harvest
- arrange fodder or water contingency
- request expert review

## 5. Confidence and expiry

Each advisory shall include:

- risk type
- affected fields
- valid period
- action window
- confidence
- source
- expiry
- reassessment trigger
- contingency option

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-158: The platform shall support monsoon and climate-risk advisories.
- BR-159: Monsoon guidance shall be probabilistic rather than guaranteed.
- BR-160: Advisories shall include affected fields, action window, source, confidence, and expiry.
- BR-161: Delayed-onset advisories shall support contingency crops or varieties.
- BR-162: Advisories shall be updated or withdrawn when conditions change.
