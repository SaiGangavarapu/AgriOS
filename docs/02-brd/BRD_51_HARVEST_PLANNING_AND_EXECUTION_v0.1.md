# BRD 51 — Harvest Planning and Execution

## 1. Objective

Plan and record harvest at suitable maturity and weather conditions while reducing loss and preserving quality.

## 2. Harvest-readiness inputs

- crop stage
- maturity indicators
- moisture
- field observations
- forecast rain
- humidity
- labour
- machinery
- buyer schedule
- storage readiness
- transport availability

## 3. Harvest plan

- field and crop cycle
- expected harvest window
- planned method
- labour and machinery
- target quality
- expected quantity
- expected losses
- weather contingency
- buyer or storage destination

## 4. Harvest execution

Record:

- actual date and time
- harvested area
- quantity
- moisture
- grade
- loss
- labour
- machinery
- cost
- weather
- destination
- lot identifiers

## 5. Partial harvest

Support:

- multiple harvest rounds
- selective picking
- field-zone harvest
- quality-based separation
- unharvested balance

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-223: The platform shall generate harvest-readiness and harvest plans.
- BR-224: Harvest plans shall consider weather, labour, machinery, buyer, and storage constraints.
- BR-225: Actual harvest shall record quantity, area, quality, moisture, loss, and cost.
- BR-226: Multiple and partial harvests shall be supported.
- BR-227: Harvest output shall create traceable produce lots.
