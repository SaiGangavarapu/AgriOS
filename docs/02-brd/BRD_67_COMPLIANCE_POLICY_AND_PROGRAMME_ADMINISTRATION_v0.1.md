# BRD 67 — Compliance, Policy, and Programme Administration

## 1. Objective

Configure jurisdictional, programme, certification, and institutional rules without hard-coding them into farmer workflows.

## 2. Configurable policy areas

- geography
- consent
- retention
- seed classification
- fertilizer and pesticide controls
- organic certification
- insurance scheme
- lender programme
- government programme
- advisory review
- language
- reporting
- audit retention

## 3. Policy lifecycle

`Draft → Reviewed → Approved → Scheduled → Active → Superseded → Retired`

## 4. Policy applicability

- jurisdiction
- programme
- organization
- crop
- product
- season
- date
- user role
- farming system

## 5. Programme administration

Support:

- eligibility
- enrolment
- participant status
- geographic coverage
- services
- funding period
- reporting indicators
- partner roles
- exit

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-303: The platform shall maintain versioned policy configuration.
- BR-304: Policies shall support jurisdiction, programme, crop, role, and date applicability.
- BR-305: Policy activation and retirement shall be auditable.
- BR-306: Programme eligibility and enrolment shall be configurable.
- BR-307: Historical transactions shall retain the policy version used.
