# BRD 03 — Vision, Goals, and Business Outcomes

## 1. Vision

Enable every participating farmer to make timely, field-specific, evidence-based agricultural decisions using understandable and trustworthy digital support.

## 2. Mission

Create an extensible agriculture operating platform that unifies farm records, agronomy, weather, IoT, experts, markets, and AI while preserving farmer control over data.

## 3. Strategic goals

### G-01: Improve decision quality

Recommendations must use field, crop, soil, water, weather, and farmer context.

### G-02: Reduce avoidable input waste

The platform should reduce unnecessary, mistimed, or duplicated applications.

### G-03: Improve soil and water stewardship

The platform should support long-term soil health and more efficient water use.

### G-04: Improve resilience

Farmers should receive contingency options for delayed rain, dry spells, flooding, heat, and other disruptions.

### G-05: Improve accessibility

The experience should be multilingual, voice-friendly, mobile-first, and offline-capable.

### G-06: Improve trust

Every recommendation should be explainable, traceable, and governed.

### G-07: Enable ecosystem coordination

Experts, laboratories, FPOs, buyers, insurers, and government partners should collaborate through consented workflows.

### G-08: Build a shared platform

Agriculture, livestock, dairy, marketplace, and ERP products should reuse common identity, consent, data, and integration services where appropriate.

## 4. Expected business outcomes

- more timely farm operations
- better input planning
- improved advisory reach
- improved record quality
- improved expert productivity
- better season-level profitability visibility
- improved evidence for insurance and lending workflows
- improved traceability for buyers and certification
- reduced duplication across agriculture applications
