# BRD 46 — Pest and Disease Knowledge

## 1. Objective

Maintain a governed knowledge base for crop pests, diseases, disorders, beneficial organisms, risk factors, scouting methods, and approved management options.

## 2. Knowledge categories

- insect pest
- mite
- nematode
- fungal disease
- bacterial disease
- viral disease
- phytoplasma disease
- nutritional disorder
- physiological disorder
- herbicide injury
- weather injury
- beneficial organism
- unknown or mixed stress

## 3. Knowledge attributes

- common name
- scientific name
- crop and variety applicability
- geographic applicability
- crop-stage applicability
- symptoms
- signs
- affected plant part
- spread pattern
- environmental conditions
- economic threshold where validated
- differential diagnoses
- preventive practices
- monitoring method
- management options
- source
- evidence grade
- review status

## 4. Governance lifecycle

`Draft → Expert Reviewed → Approved → Published → Superseded → Withdrawn`

## 5. Commercial separation

Product names may be mapped to approved active ingredients or treatments, but commercial claims shall not replace agronomic evidence.

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-198: The platform shall maintain governed pest, disease, disorder, and beneficial-organism knowledge.
- BR-199: Knowledge shall be crop-, stage-, geography-, and evidence-scoped.
- BR-200: Differential diagnoses shall be retained.
- BR-201: Economic thresholds shall only be used where validated.
- BR-202: Superseded and withdrawn knowledge shall remain auditable.
