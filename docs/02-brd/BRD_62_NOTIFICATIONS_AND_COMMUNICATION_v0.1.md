# BRD 62 — Notifications and Communication

## 1. Objective

Deliver timely, understandable, prioritized communications through channels suitable for the farmer and risk level.

## 2. Channels

- in-app
- push notification
- SMS
- voice call or IVR where supported
- WhatsApp or approved messaging integration
- email
- field-officer task
- printable notice
- organization broadcast

## 3. Notification categories

- critical weather warning
- crop-risk advisory
- task reminder
- expert response
- device alert
- consent expiry
- visit reminder
- payment or buyer update
- system maintenance
- educational message

## 4. Priority

- Critical
- High
- Normal
- Low
- Informational

## 5. Delivery record

- message
- language
- recipient
- channel
- scheduled time
- sent time
- delivery status
- failure reason
- acknowledgement
- expiry
- source event

## 6. Communication preferences

Farmers may configure:

- preferred language
- preferred channel
- quiet hours
- voice preference
- non-critical opt-out

Critical safety and legally required messages may follow separate rules.

## 7. Business requirements

- BR-278: The platform shall support multi-channel notifications.
- BR-279: Notifications shall support priority, expiry, and acknowledgement.
- BR-280: Channel fallback shall be configurable for critical alerts.
- BR-281: Communication preferences shall be respected for non-critical content.
- BR-282: Delivery outcomes shall be auditable.
