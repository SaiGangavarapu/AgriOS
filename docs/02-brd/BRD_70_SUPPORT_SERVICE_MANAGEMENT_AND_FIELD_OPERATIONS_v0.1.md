# BRD 70 — Support, Service Management, and Field Operations

## 1. Objective

Provide reliable support for farmers, field officers, experts, devices, integrations, and institutional users.

## 2. Support channels

- in-app help
- phone
- field officer
- messaging
- email
- institution help desk
- device technician

## 3. Case categories

- login
- farmer or field data
- agronomy clarification
- wrong advisory
- device
- synchronization
- payment or buyer
- consent or privacy
- insurance or lending
- safety
- complaint

## 4. Case lifecycle

`Created → Triaged → Assigned → In Progress → Waiting for User/Partner → Resolved → Confirmed → Closed`

## 5. Service levels

Service levels may vary by:

- severity
- safety impact
- crop stage
- user type
- programme
- geography
- channel
- support hours

## 6. Knowledge and escalation

Support should use:

- approved knowledge articles
- standard operating procedures
- expert escalation
- engineering escalation
- security escalation
- device technician dispatch

## 7. Business requirements

- BR-318: The platform shall support multi-channel support cases.
- BR-319: Support cases shall have severity, owner, status, and service level.
- BR-320: Agronomy and safety questions shall support expert escalation.
- BR-321: Device cases shall link device, installation, telemetry, and maintenance history.
- BR-322: Support quality and recurring issues shall be reportable.
