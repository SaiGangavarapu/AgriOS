# BRD 54 — Farm Economics and Profitability

## 1. Objective

Provide transparent field- and crop-cycle-level cost, revenue, margin, and scenario analysis.

## 2. Cost categories

- seed and planting material
- fertilizer and amendment
- crop protection
- labour
- machinery
- irrigation
- electricity and fuel
- rent or lease
- transport
- storage
- finance cost
- insurance
- certification
- post-harvest
- miscellaneous

## 3. Revenue categories

- produce sale
- by-product sale
- household consumption value
- seed retained
- fodder retained
- insurance compensation
- programme incentive

## 4. Economic outputs

- total variable cost
- total allocated cost
- revenue
- gross margin
- net margin where sufficient data exists
- cost per unit
- break-even price
- break-even yield
- price realization
- loss estimate
- cash-flow requirement

## 5. Scenario analysis

- expected yield
- low yield
- high yield
- price downside
- input-price increase
- delayed sale
- partial crop loss

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-238: The platform shall maintain crop-cycle costs and revenues.
- BR-239: Missing, estimated, allocated, and confirmed financial values shall be distinguishable.
- BR-240: The platform shall calculate margin, break-even price, and break-even yield.
- BR-241: Scenario analysis shall support yield and price variation.
- BR-242: Financial reports shall not be presented as audited accounts unless formally validated.
