# BRD 10 — Business Risks

## R-01: Incorrect agronomic recommendation

Impact: crop loss, financial loss, safety issue.

Mitigation:

- evidence governance
- deterministic rules
- expert review
- confidence
- audit
- restricted automation

## R-02: Farmer over-trust

Impact: farmer follows advice without local judgment.

Mitigation:

- explain uncertainty
- show source
- provide escalation
- avoid guarantee language

## R-03: Commercial influence

Impact: unnecessary input purchase.

Mitigation:

- separate marketplace and recommendation services
- disclose sponsorship
- independent governance

## R-04: Data misuse

Impact: loss of trust, discrimination, regulatory exposure.

Mitigation:

- granular consent
- minimum necessary sharing
- audit
- revocation
- purpose limitation

## R-05: Sensor inaccuracy

Impact: wrong irrigation or nutrient decision.

Mitigation:

- calibration
- health monitoring
- representative placement
- quality score
- laboratory precedence

## R-06: Forecast misunderstanding

Impact: mistimed sowing, irrigation, spraying, or harvest.

Mitigation:

- probability and confidence
- multiple horizons
- official warnings
- update and expiry

## R-07: Digital exclusion

Impact: vulnerable farmers receive less benefit.

Mitigation:

- field officer mode
- voice
- local language
- offline
- shared access
- assisted onboarding

## R-08: Scope expansion

Impact: delayed delivery and unstable architecture.

Mitigation:

- pilot boundaries
- phased BRD
- architecture decisions
- controlled backlog

## R-09: Insufficient expert capacity

Impact: review queues become unusable.

Mitigation:

- risk-based routing
- reusable approved rules
- service-level targets
- institutional partnerships

## R-10: Weak economic value

Impact: farmers do not continue using the platform.

Mitigation:

- focus on measurable decisions
- avoid unnecessary devices
- pilot benefit tracking
- low-cost deployment options
