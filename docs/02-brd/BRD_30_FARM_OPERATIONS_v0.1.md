# BRD 30 — Farm Operations

## 1. Objective

Plan, assign, record, and evaluate activities performed on a field or crop cycle.

## 2. Operation categories

- land preparation
- sowing
- irrigation
- nutrient application
- weeding
- interculture
- pest management
- disease management
- pruning or training
- scouting
- harvest
- post-harvest
- soil or water sampling
- maintenance

## 3. Operation record

- crop cycle
- field or zone
- operation type
- planned date
- actual date
- operator
- labour
- machinery
- input
- quantity
- unit
- cost
- weather
- result
- photo
- notes
- source of instruction

## 4. Operation status

`Planned → Assigned → Started → Completed → Verified`

Exceptional states:

- Cancelled
- Skipped
- Failed
- Rework Required
- Partially Completed

## 5. Input-use controls

The platform should validate:

- allowed crop and stage
- quantity
- unit
- duplication
- application interval
- weather restriction
- safety instruction
- product status

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-117: The platform shall plan and record farm operations.
- BR-118: Operations shall support field-zone specificity.
- BR-119: Labour, machinery, inputs, and cost shall be recorded.
- BR-120: Input applications shall support safety validation.
- BR-121: Failed and partial operations shall support follow-up.
