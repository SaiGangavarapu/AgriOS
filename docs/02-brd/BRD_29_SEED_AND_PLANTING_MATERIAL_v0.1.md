# BRD 29 — Seed and Planting Material

## 1. Objective

Ensure seed and planting material are suitable, traceable, sufficient, and correctly used.

## 2. Material types

- certified seed
- foundation seed
- breeder seed
- truthfully labelled seed
- hybrid seed
- farmer-saved seed
- seedlings
- cuttings
- tubers
- bulbs
- grafts
- other vegetative material

## 3. Lot attributes

- crop and variety
- supplier
- producer
- lot
- certification
- pack date
- expiry
- germination
- purity
- treatment
- storage
- quantity
- invoice

## 4. Requirement calculation

The system should consider:

- target population
- germination
- purity
- establishment loss
- seed size
- spacing
- sowing method
- reserve quantity

## 5. Farmer-saved seed

Support:

- source field and season
- storage
- germination test
- identity confidence
- disease observations

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-112: The platform shall maintain seed-lot traceability.
- BR-113: Seed requirement calculations shall expose assumptions.
- BR-114: Farmer-saved seed shall support quality checks.
- BR-115: Expired or low-quality seed shall generate warnings.
- BR-116: Seed treatment records shall be linked to the lot and crop cycle.
