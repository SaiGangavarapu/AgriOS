# BRD 52 — Post-Harvest, Grading, and Storage

## 1. Objective

Maintain produce quality, reduce loss, and preserve lot identity after harvest.

## 2. Post-harvest activities

- drying
- cleaning
- threshing
- sorting
- grading
- curing
- washing
- packing
- cooling
- storage
- fumigation or pest management where permitted
- transport preparation

## 3. Grade profile

- commodity
- grade
- quality attributes
- moisture range
- size
- colour
- defects
- contamination
- buyer standard
- certification standard

## 4. Storage profile

- facility
- location
- capacity
- storage type
- temperature
- humidity
- pest controls
- lot positions
- inspection schedule
- owner
- operator

## 5. Loss recording

- quantity loss
- quality downgrade
- cause
- date
- affected lot
- evidence
- financial impact
- corrective action

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-228: The platform shall record post-harvest operations.
- BR-229: Produce shall support grade and quality classification.
- BR-230: Storage shall maintain lot location and condition history.
- BR-231: Quantity loss and quality downgrade shall be recorded separately.
- BR-232: Storage alerts shall support inspection and corrective action.
