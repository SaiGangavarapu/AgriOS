# BRD 47 — Field Scouting and Observations

## 1. Objective

Support repeatable field inspection and structured recording of crop-health observations.

## 2. Scouting plan

- field and crop cycle
- crop stage
- purpose
- route or pattern
- sample count
- plant parts to inspect
- trap or observation points
- due window
- assigned person

## 3. Observation attributes

- date and time
- observer
- location
- field zone
- crop stage
- symptom category
- plant part
- severity
- incidence
- distribution pattern
- pest count
- trap count
- weather
- notes
- photos
- confidence

## 4. Severity and incidence

The platform shall distinguish:

- severity on affected plant
- incidence across sampled plants
- affected area
- estimated yield risk

## 5. Scouting lifecycle

`Planned → Assigned → Started → Completed → Reviewed → Closed`

## 6. Escalation triggers

- rapid spread
- high severity
- unknown symptom
- suspected quarantine or regulated pest
- repeated treatment failure
- crop-wide impact
- food-safety concern

## 7. Business requirements

- BR-203: The platform shall support planned and ad hoc scouting.
- BR-204: Observations shall be field-zone and crop-stage aware.
- BR-205: Severity and incidence shall be recorded separately.
- BR-206: Scouting shall support photos, counts, and geolocation.
- BR-207: High-risk observations shall trigger escalation.
