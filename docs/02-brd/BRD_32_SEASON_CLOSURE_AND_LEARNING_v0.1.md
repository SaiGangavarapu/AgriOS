# BRD 32 — Season Closure and Learning

## 1. Objective

Close the crop cycle with complete production, cost, quality, outcome, and learning records.

## 2. Closure inputs

- harvest quantity
- harvest area
- grade
- moisture
- losses
- household use
- seed retained
- fodder retained
- sales
- unsold stock
- total inputs
- labour
- machinery
- irrigation
- crop damage
- insurance event
- farmer feedback

## 3. Outcome classification

- successful
- partially successful
- loss
- abandoned
- data incomplete

## 4. Calculations

- yield per area
- total cost
- revenue
- gross margin
- price realization
- water-use estimate
- nutrient-input summary
- loss estimate

## 5. Learning record

- what worked
- what failed
- weather impact
- pest or disease impact
- variety performance
- soil or water constraint
- farmer preference
- expert conclusion
- recommendation for next season

## 6. Business requirements

- BR-127: The platform shall support formal crop-cycle closure.
- BR-128: Closure shall include production, cost, quality, and loss.
- BR-129: Season outcomes shall be comparable across years.
- BR-130: Learning shall feed future crop planning.
- BR-131: Incomplete closure shall remain distinguishable from zero values.
