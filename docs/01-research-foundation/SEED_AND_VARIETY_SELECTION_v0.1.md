# Seed and Variety Selection

## 1. Objective

Select seed that is agronomically suitable, legally acceptable, available, traceable, affordable, and aligned with farmer and market needs.

## 2. Seed classes and concepts

The platform should distinguish:

- breeder seed
- foundation seed
- certified seed
- truthfully labelled seed where applicable
- hybrid seed
- open-pollinated variety
- farmer-saved seed
- vegetative planting material

The exact regulatory classification must be configurable by jurisdiction and crop.

## 3. Variety attributes

- crop species
- variety name
- release or notification status
- breeder or institution
- maturity duration
- recommended geography
- season
- irrigation suitability
- soil constraints
- disease reaction
- pest reaction
- drought tolerance
- flood or submergence tolerance
- heat tolerance
- salinity tolerance
- lodging
- grain or produce quality
- processing quality
- market acceptance
- seed rate
- spacing
- sowing method

## 4. Lot-level seed traceability

Capture:

- supplier
- producer
- lot number
- certification details
- test date
- germination
- purity
- treatment
- pack date
- expiry
- quantity
- invoice
- storage condition

## 5. Ranking model

Candidate varieties should be scored on:

- geographic recommendation
- soil fit
- sowing-window fit
- water fit
- duration
- weather risk
- resistance traits
- market fit
- local performance evidence
- seed availability
- cost
- farmer preference

Hard constraints should eliminate unsafe or unsupported options before scoring.

## 6. Seed-rate calculation

Must consider:

- recommended plant population
- germination percentage
- physical purity
- expected establishment loss
- seed size
- sowing method
- row and plant spacing

The calculation and assumptions must be visible.

## 7. Seed treatment

The knowledge base should classify:

- physical treatment
- biological treatment
- chemical treatment
- inoculation

Treatment recommendations require crop, seed status, disease risk, product registration, compatibility, and safety checks.

## 8. Farmer-saved seed

Record:

- source season
- field
- variety identity confidence
- storage
- germination test
- disease observations
- selection method

The system should recommend a germination test before sowing where quality is uncertain.
