# Subdomain Classification

## Core subdomains

### Farm and Field Digital Twin

Maintains the authoritative operational representation of farms, fields, boundaries, water sources, field versions, and active agricultural state.

### Crop Planning

Evaluates candidate crops, varieties, farming systems, resource constraints, and farmer goals.

### Crop Cycle

Owns the actual lifecycle of a crop on a field.

### Advisory

Converts validated domain state and evidence into explainable action guidance.

### Agronomy Knowledge

Owns approved crop, variety, soil, nutrient, irrigation, pest, disease, and treatment knowledge.

### Soil and Water Intelligence

Owns sample, laboratory, interpretation, constraints, and validity.

### Nutrient Management

Owns nutrient plans, nutrient budgets, application constraints, and plan revisions.

### Irrigation Management

Owns irrigation schedules, execution, and water accounting.
