# SA 01 — Architecture Principles

1. Farmer workflows remain simple; complexity stays behind role-gated administration.
2. Begin as a modular monolith.
3. Bounded contexts own their domain model, data, APIs, and events.
4. No cross-context table access.
5. Official and expert-reviewed sources outrank commercial or inferred data.
6. LLMs explain and orchestrate; they do not invent agronomy.
7. Laboratory results remain authoritative for nutrient status.
8. IoT data always carries quality and calibration state.
9. Safety-critical automation requires manual override and interlocks.
10. Offline operation is a first-class requirement.
11. Consent and purpose limitation apply to every data share.
12. External systems enter through anti-corruption layers.
13. Canonical identifiers and units are used internally.
14. Projections are rebuildable and not authoritative.
15. Architecture tests enforce boundaries continuously.
