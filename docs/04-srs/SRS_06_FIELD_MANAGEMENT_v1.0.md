# SRS 06 — Field Management

- FR-FIELD-001: Register fields under farms.
- FR-FIELD-002: Capture GPS, map-drawn, or imported boundaries.
- FR-FIELD-003: Store geometry method, accuracy, actor, time, and coordinate system.
- FR-FIELD-004: Validate closure, self-intersection, overlap, location, and area.
- FR-FIELD-005: Version approved boundaries immutably.
- FR-FIELD-006: Support field split and merge with lineage.
- FR-FIELD-007: Support management zones.
- FR-FIELD-008: Restrict exact geometry by role and consent.
- FR-FIELD-009: Link crop cycles to the applicable field version.
