# SRS 17 — Weather and Monsoon

- FR-WEATHER-001: Ingest official, local-station, commercial, satellite, and farmer-observation data.
- FR-WEATHER-002: Store provider, issue time, valid time, geometry, variable, unit, probability, and quality.
- FR-WEATHER-003: Support nowcast, short, medium, extended, seasonal, and historical horizons.
- FR-WEATHER-004: Preserve forecast disagreement.
- FR-WEATHER-005: Prioritize official warnings.
- FR-WEATHER-006: Generate sowing, irrigation, fertilizer, spray, heat, flood, dry-spell, and harvest advisories.
- FR-WEATHER-007: Present monsoon guidance probabilistically with confidence and contingency.
