# SRS 28 — Reporting

Required reports:

- farmer, farm, field, tenure, and consent
- soil and water status
- crop plans and active crop cycles
- tasks and operations
- nutrient and irrigation schedules
- weather and advisories
- device health and telemetry completeness
- crop-health cases and treatments
- harvest, storage, and lot balances
- market offers and economics
- insurance, lending, audit, and support

All reports shall support role, tenant, programme, geography, date, crop, field, consent, export audit, and data-quality annotations.
