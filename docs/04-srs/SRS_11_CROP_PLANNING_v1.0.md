# SRS 11 — Crop Planning

- FR-PLAN-001: Rank multiple crops and varieties for field and season.
- FR-PLAN-002: Apply hard constraints before scoring.
- FR-PLAN-003: Consider soil, water, climate, sowing window, rotation, budget, labour, machinery, market, food, and fodder needs.
- FR-PLAN-004: Show suitability, confidence, cost, yield, price scenarios, risks, and contingency.
- FR-PLAN-005: Compare conventional, integrated, organic, and natural pathways where evidence exists.
- FR-PLAN-006: Require explicit farmer selection and plan approval.
- FR-PLAN-007: Preserve revisions and rationale.
