# SRS 25 — Economics

- FR-ECON-001: Record seed, nutrient, crop-protection, labour, machinery, irrigation, energy, rent, transport, storage, finance, and insurance costs.
- FR-ECON-002: Record sale, by-product, household use, retained seed, fodder, compensation, and incentive revenue.
- FR-ECON-003: Distinguish missing, estimated, allocated, and confirmed values.
- FR-ECON-004: Calculate cost, revenue, gross margin, break-even price, break-even yield, and price realization.
- FR-ECON-005: Run low, expected, and high yield/price scenarios.
- FR-ECON-006: Avoid presenting outputs as audited accounts unless formally validated.
