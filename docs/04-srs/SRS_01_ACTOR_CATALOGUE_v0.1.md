# SRS 01 — Actor Catalogue

## A-001 Farmer

Primary actor who owns or operates farm activities.

Capabilities:

- manage own profile
- view farms and fields
- approve crop plans
- acknowledge advisories
- record operations
- manage consent
- delegate access

## A-002 Household Operator

Acts on behalf of a farmer under explicit delegation.

## A-003 Farm Worker

Records assigned tasks and operations within granted scope.

## A-004 Field Officer

Performs assisted onboarding, field visits, boundary capture, and follow-up.

## A-005 Agronomist

Reviews agronomy cases and advisories.

## A-006 Soil or Water Laboratory Operator

Receives samples and publishes test results.

## A-007 FPO or Organization Manager

Manages membership and organization-scoped services.

## A-008 System Administrator

Manages platform configuration, roles, reference data, and operational settings.

## A-009 Security Administrator

Manages security policy, incident response, and privileged access.

## A-010 Support Operator

Handles user and device support cases.

## A-011 External Identity Provider

Provides authentication or identity assertions.

## A-012 External Government System

Provides programme, geography, or scheme data.

## A-013 External Laboratory System

Provides laboratory orders and test results.

## A-014 External Weather Provider

Provides observations, forecasts, and warnings.

## A-015 External Buyer, Insurer, or Lender

Consumes consented business data through governed interfaces.
