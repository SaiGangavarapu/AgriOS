# SRS 11 — Tenure and Cultivation Rights

## FR-TENURE-001 Record ownership declaration

The system shall support ownership declaration without treating it as legal title validation.

## FR-TENURE-002 Record lease

The system shall record lease dates, parties, evidence, and restrictions.

## FR-TENURE-003 Record sharecropping

The system shall record sharecropping arrangements and configured share terms.

## FR-TENURE-004 Record cultivation right

The system shall maintain active cultivation authority separately from ownership.

## FR-TENURE-005 End tenure

The system shall end tenure without deleting history.

## FR-TENURE-006 Dispute

The system shall support disputed status and restrict sensitive sharing.

## VR-TENURE-001 Overlap

Conflicting active cultivation rights shall trigger review.

## VR-TENURE-002 Expiry

Expired tenure shall not authorize a new crop cycle.

## SR-TENURE-001 Evidence access

Tenure documents shall be restricted to permitted roles.

## DR-TENURE-001 Historical validity

The system shall preserve tenure validity periods and evidence references.
