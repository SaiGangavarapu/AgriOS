# SRS 09 — Crop Knowledge

- FR-KNOW-001: Maintain governed crop and variety catalogues.
- FR-KNOW-002: Store duration, stage model, climate, soil, water, nutrient, pest, disease, harvest, and quality attributes.
- FR-KNOW-003: Store variety release status, geography, season, tolerance, resistance, and market traits.
- FR-KNOW-004: Require evidence source, grade, reviewer, validity, and applicability.
- FR-KNOW-005: Support Draft, Reviewed, Approved, Published, Superseded, and Withdrawn states.
- FR-KNOW-006: Keep commercial claims distinguishable from independent evidence.
