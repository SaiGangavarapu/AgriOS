# SRS 24 — Traceability

- FR-TRACE-001: Create lots from harvest.
- FR-TRACE-002: Preserve field, crop cycle, variety, harvest, quality, and ownership links.
- FR-TRACE-003: Support split, merge, drying, cleaning, grading, repacking, processing handoff, and transfer.
- FR-TRACE-004: Validate quantity balances.
- FR-TRACE-005: Block unsupported organic, natural, certification, or quality claims.
- FR-TRACE-006: Enforce consent and commercial confidentiality.
