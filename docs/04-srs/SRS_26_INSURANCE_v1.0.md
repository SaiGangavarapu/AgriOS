# SRS 26 — Insurance

- FR-INS-001: Store insurer, scheme, policy, field, crop, area, period, value, premium, exclusions, and status.
- FR-INS-002: Record loss event, date, field, stage, area, weather, images, observations, and expert evidence.
- FR-INS-003: Prepare evidence package with provenance and verification level.
- FR-INS-004: Require farmer approval before submission.
- FR-INS-005: Track external review and decision.
- FR-INS-006: Never autonomously approve or reject claims.
