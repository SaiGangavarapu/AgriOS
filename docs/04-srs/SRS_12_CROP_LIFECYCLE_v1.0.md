# SRS 12 — Crop Lifecycle

- FR-CYCLE-001: Support lifecycle from Planned through Closed.
- FR-CYCLE-002: Support cancelled, failed establishment, re-sowing, partial loss, total loss, and abandoned states.
- FR-CYCLE-003: Track field allocation and prevent silent over-allocation.
- FR-CYCLE-004: Support intercropping and partial-field geometry.
- FR-CYCLE-005: Track crop stage with source and confidence.
- FR-CYCLE-006: Re-evaluate tasks when stage changes.
- FR-CYCLE-007: Support season closure with yield, quality, cost, revenue, loss, and learning.
