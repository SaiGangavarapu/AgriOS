# SRS 23 — Marketplace

- FR-MARKET-001: Ingest market prices with source, date, commodity, variety, grade, unit, and quality.
- FR-MARKET-002: Register buyer profiles and requirements.
- FR-MARKET-003: Compare gross price, grading deduction, transport, commission, payment delay, and rejection risk.
- FR-MARKET-004: Estimate net realization.
- FR-MARKET-005: Support offer, negotiation, acceptance, delivery, quality confirmation, payment, and closure.
- FR-MARKET-006: Keep commercial offers separate from agronomic ranking.
