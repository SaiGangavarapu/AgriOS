# SRS 05 — Farm Management

- FR-FARM-001: Register multiple farms per farmer.
- FR-FARM-002: Support multiple farm participants and roles.
- FR-FARM-003: Record farm type, location, infrastructure, water sources, and status.
- FR-FARM-004: Support verification with method and evidence.
- FR-FARM-005: Support Draft, Registered, Verified, Active, Inactive, and Archived states.
- FR-FARM-006: Prevent archive when active crop cycles exist.
- FR-FARM-007: Preserve historical operations after status change.
- FR-FARM-008: Support farm-level assets and infrastructure references.
