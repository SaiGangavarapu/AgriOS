# SRS 20 — Pest and Disease

- FR-HEALTH-001: Maintain governed pest, disease, disorder, and beneficial-organism knowledge.
- FR-HEALTH-002: Support structured scouting by field, zone, stage, sample, severity, incidence, count, and distribution.
- FR-HEALTH-003: Support guided image capture with metadata and quality.
- FR-HEALTH-004: Return differential diagnoses and confidence.
- FR-HEALTH-005: Distinguish farmer, AI, expert, and laboratory findings.
- FR-HEALTH-006: Support preventive, cultural, mechanical, biological, and approved chemical controls.
- FR-HEALTH-007: Enforce legal status, dose, interval, PPE, weather, PHI, REI, and resistance rules.
- FR-HEALTH-008: Record effectiveness and adverse effects.
