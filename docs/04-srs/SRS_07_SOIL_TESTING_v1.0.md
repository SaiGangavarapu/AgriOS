# SRS 07 — Soil Testing

- FR-SOIL-001: Plan representative sampling by field and zone.
- FR-SOIL-002: Record depth, cores, pattern, recent inputs, crop, and collector.
- FR-SOIL-003: Maintain chain of custody.
- FR-SOIL-004: Record laboratory method, value, unit, quality, and reviewer.
- FR-SOIL-005: Support pH, EC, organic carbon, macro- and micronutrients.
- FR-SOIL-006: Interpret results using versioned geography-, crop-, and method-specific rules.
- FR-SOIL-007: Mark tests current, stale, invalid, rejected, or recollection-required.
- FR-SOIL-008: Prevent low-cost sensors from silently overriding laboratory results.
