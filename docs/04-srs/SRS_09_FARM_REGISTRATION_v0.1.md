# SRS 09 — Farm Registration

## FR-FARM-001 Register farm

The system shall allow an authorized farmer or assisted actor to register a farm.

Required data:

- farm name or local identifier
- primary operator
- location
- farm type
- operational status

## FR-FARM-002 Add participants

The system shall support multiple farm participants with roles.

## FR-FARM-003 Verify farm

The system shall support verification using configured methods.

## FR-FARM-004 Farm infrastructure

The system shall support references to storage, machinery, energy, irrigation, and livestock infrastructure.

## FR-FARM-005 Multiple farms

A farmer shall be able to participate in multiple farms.

## FR-FARM-006 Farm lifecycle

The system shall support Draft, Registered, Verified, Active, Inactive, and Archived states.

## VR-FARM-001 Primary operator

An active farm shall have at least one active primary operator.

## VR-FARM-002 Archive

A farm with active crop cycles shall not be archived.

## SR-FARM-001 Farm scope

Farm updates shall require farm-scoped authorization.

## DR-FARM-001 Verification provenance

Farm verification method and evidence shall be retained.
