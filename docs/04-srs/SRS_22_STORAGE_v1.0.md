# SRS 22 — Storage

- FR-STORAGE-001: Record drying, cleaning, sorting, grading, curing, packing, cooling, and storage operations.
- FR-STORAGE-002: Maintain storage facility, capacity, conditions, operator, and inspection schedule.
- FR-STORAGE-003: Track lot location and movement.
- FR-STORAGE-004: Record quantity loss and quality downgrade separately.
- FR-STORAGE-005: Alert on capacity, temperature, humidity, pest, or inspection issues.
- FR-STORAGE-006: Preserve post-harvest lineage.
