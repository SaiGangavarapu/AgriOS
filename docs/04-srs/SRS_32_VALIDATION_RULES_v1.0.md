# SRS 32 — Validation Rules

Validation classes:

- Blocking
- Warning
- Review Required

Key rules:

- farmer duplicate and version conflict
- field geometry and overlap
- tenure expiry and conflict
- soil/water method, unit, validity, and quality
- crop hard constraints and field allocation
- seed quantity and expiry
- nutrient rate, evidence, duplication, compatibility, and weather
- irrigation source, runtime, flow, and safety
- stale or inconsistent weather
- device calibration and telemetry quality
- advisory evidence, confidence, expiry, and contradiction
- treatment legality, dose, interval, PHI, REI, PPE, and weather
- harvest quantity and maturity
- storage capacity and lot balance
- consent purpose, recipient, scope, and expiry
