# SRS 08 — Water Testing

- FR-WATER-001: Register samples against water sources.
- FR-WATER-002: Record pH, EC, TDS, sodium, calcium, magnesium, bicarbonate, chloride, nitrate, and configured contaminants.
- FR-WATER-003: Evaluate salinity, sodicity, infiltration, toxicity, and crop risk.
- FR-WATER-004: Link validity to source, pumping depth, season, and fields.
- FR-WATER-005: Trigger expert escalation for critical results.
- FR-WATER-006: Retain historical water-quality trends.
