# SRS 19 — Advisory AI

- FR-AI-001: Use validated domain tools and approved knowledge.
- FR-AI-002: Provide action, timing, quantity, reason, risk of delay, confidence, evidence, and expiry.
- FR-AI-003: Use LLMs for interaction, explanation, translation, extraction, and summarization.
- FR-AI-004: Prevent LLMs from inventing field-specific agronomic prescriptions.
- FR-AI-005: Require confirmation for extracted high-impact values.
- FR-AI-006: Escalate low-confidence and high-risk cases.
- FR-AI-007: Retain tool calls, source versions, and advisory rationale.
- FR-AI-008: Support multilingual text and voice.
