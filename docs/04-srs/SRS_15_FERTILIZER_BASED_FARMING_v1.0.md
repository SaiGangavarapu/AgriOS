# SRS 15 — Fertilizer-Based Farming

- FR-FERT-001: Base recommendations on soil test, crop, stage, target yield, irrigation, and credits.
- FR-FERT-002: Support N, P, K, S, Ca, Mg, and configured micronutrients.
- FR-FERT-003: Prevent blanket recommendations where field data exists.
- FR-FERT-004: Detect duplicate, excessive, incompatible, expired, or mistimed applications.
- FR-FERT-005: Enforce rain, saturation, wind, and runoff lockouts.
- FR-FERT-006: Record product lot, nutrient analysis, quantity, method, operator, equipment, and cost.
- FR-FERT-007: Preserve application and correction history.
