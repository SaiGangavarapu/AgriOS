# SRS 13 — Nutrient Management

- FR-NUTRIENT-001: Generate nutrient budgets using soil supply, credits, additions, crop removal, and losses.
- FR-NUTRIENT-002: Support source, rate, timing, and placement planning.
- FR-NUTRIENT-003: Convert nutrient requirement to product quantity transparently.
- FR-NUTRIENT-004: Support basal, split, foliar, and fertigation schedules.
- FR-NUTRIENT-005: Validate crop stage, soil, weather, interval, source compatibility, and certification.
- FR-NUTRIENT-006: Require evidence for micronutrients.
- FR-NUTRIENT-007: Compare cost, labour, adequacy, uncertainty, and soil-health contribution.
