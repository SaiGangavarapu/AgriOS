# SRS 14 — Fertilizer-Free Farming

- FR-NATURAL-001: Support compost, farmyard manure, vermicompost, green manure, residues, mulching, legumes, cover crops, and biofertilizers.
- FR-NATURAL-002: Quantify available biomass and likely nutrient contribution.
- FR-NATURAL-003: Record compost maturity and source quality.
- FR-NATURAL-004: Track nutrient export in harvested produce.
- FR-NATURAL-005: Support transition plans rather than assume immediate full replacement.
- FR-NATURAL-006: Show yield, labour, nutrient sufficiency, and transition uncertainty.
- FR-NATURAL-007: Support organic-certification restrictions and records separately from general natural farming.
