# SRS 03 — Use Cases

Core use-case groups:

- farmer onboarding and verification
- household and organization membership
- farm and field registration
- tenure and water-source registration
- soil and water testing
- crop suitability and plan approval
- seed allocation
- crop-cycle activation
- operation and task completion
- nutrient and irrigation planning
- weather and monsoon advisory
- IoT installation and telemetry
- advisory review and publication
- pest and disease management
- harvest, lot creation, storage, and sale
- economics, insurance, and lending evidence
- reporting, consent, support, and administration
