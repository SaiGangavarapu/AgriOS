# SRS 10 — Seed Management

- FR-SEED-001: Register certified, hybrid, open-pollinated, farmer-saved, and vegetative material lots.
- FR-SEED-002: Store supplier, producer, lot, germination, purity, treatment, pack date, expiry, quantity, and invoice.
- FR-SEED-003: Calculate seed requirement from population, germination, purity, spacing, method, and establishment loss.
- FR-SEED-004: Support germination tests for farmer-saved seed.
- FR-SEED-005: Track allocation to crop cycles.
- FR-SEED-006: Prevent allocation above available quantity.
- FR-SEED-007: Link treatment records to lot and crop cycle.
