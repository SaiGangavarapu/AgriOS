# AgriOS Advisory, Expert Review, and Farmer Notification Core v1.0

Implemented:

- advisory authoring, review, approval, publication, withdrawal, and validity
- farmer, field, farm, and crop-cycle targeting
- advisory confidence, evidence snapshot, and reason codes
- actionable advisory steps
- expert profiles
- expert review cases, assignment, notes, decisions, and work queues
- optional advisory linkage from expert decisions
- in-app, SMS, WhatsApp, email, and voice notification records
- notification idempotency
- delivery attempts, provider references, retries, failure capture, and read state
- farmer advisory and notification history
- Flyway migrations V022 through V024
