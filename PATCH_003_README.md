# Patch 003 — Agriculture Platform BRD Part 1

## Purpose

Patch 003 begins the formal AgriOS Business Requirements Document. It establishes the business context, vision, stakeholder model, personas, scope, capability map, business outcomes, success measures, assumptions, constraints, and initial business-risk register.

## Included documents

- BRD index and document control
- Executive summary
- Business context and problem statement
- Vision, goals, and business outcomes
- Stakeholder model
- Farmer and institutional personas
- Scope and exclusions
- Business capability map
- Success measures and KPIs
- Assumptions, dependencies, and constraints
- Business risks and mitigations
- Initial requirements traceability register

## Apply

Extract this ZIP into the AgriOS repository root. All paths are repository-relative and the archive contains no wrapper directory.
